About Us

Where comfort meets security, and every day feels like home

Sree Lakshya is a premium ladies PG offering a safe, hygienic, and comfortable living experience for working professionals and students. Located in the heart of Bangalore, we provide a home away from home with world-class amenities and round-the-clock security. Thoughtfully designed with spacious rooms and open common areas, Sree Lakshya offers a calm, uncluttered living experience rarely found in city PGs.
